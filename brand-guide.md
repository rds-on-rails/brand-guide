## Design Notes

- **Style:** Functional travel portal вЂ” content-first, not heavily designed
- **Theme:** Light mode with blue top bar and dark gray footer
- **Layout:** WooCommerce shop grid вЂ” sidebar categories + product cards
- **Imagery:** Destination photography as trip card thumbnails + hero panorama
- **Animations:** Minimal вЂ” standard WooCommerce/WordPress interactions
- **Key Visual Elements:** "SALE" badges (red), wishlist hearts, price strikethroughs for discounts
- **Target Audience:** Indian expats/travelers living in Belgium, Luxembourg, Netherlands, and Germany
- **Tone:** Friendly, budget-conscious ("safe, economic & friendly")
